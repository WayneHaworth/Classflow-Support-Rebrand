Connecting Students' iPads to ClassFlow

ClassFlow allows you to connect students' iPads to your lesson and send lesson content. With the ClassFlow Student iPad app, students can review at their own pace, respond to poll questions, and even annotate, manipulate, and create lesson content (including taking pictures, making collages, labeling images, sorting, and more).
